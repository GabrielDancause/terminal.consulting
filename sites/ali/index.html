Hey, I'm Ali!

I'm a thirty-something Canadian nomad who traded the cold for a life of travel, curiosity, and way too many conversations about sex. I started my YouTube channel in 2022 thinking I'd talk about cooking and travel — but it quickly turned into casual, honest chats about sex, relationships, and all the stuff we're secretly curious about.

Think of me as that friend who has some experience and is genuinely open to sharing and talking about the weird, fun, and sometimes awkward side of intimacy. I'm a sex educator in training, and my goal is to make these topics feel normal, approachable, and a little bit funny.

When I'm not in front of a camera, you'll probably find me working out, cooking something delicious, or having a minor existential crisis (it happens to the best of us).
