Module 1

The Hard Limits Audit

Identify your non-negotiable boundaries as a couple. These are the only rigid rules — everything else is negotiable.

How to Use This Worksheet

  1. Sit down separately. Do not discuss beforehand.
  2. Each partner writes their 3 absolute hard limits. These are the things that are 100% non-negotiable for you.
  3. Come back together and share your lists.
  4. Find the overlap — the limits you both agree on become your shared rules.
  5. Everything else is open for discussion and negotiation.
