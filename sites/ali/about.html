My Story

I'm a thirty-something Canadian who traded Montreal winters for a life of travel, curiosity, and way too many conversations about sex. In 2022, I started a YouTube channel thinking I'd talk about cooking and travel. That lasted about two videos before I found myself talking about the stuff I actually wanted to talk about: sex, relationships, intimacy, and all the things we're curious about but don't always know how to bring up.

Turns out, a lot of people were curious too. What started as casual conversations quickly grew into a community of people who wanted honest, judgment-free takes on the topics that actually matter in our relationships and bedrooms. No scripts, no filters, just real talk.

I'm not a therapist or a doctor. Think of me as that friend who's done some research, has some experience, and is genuinely open to exploring the weird, fun, and sometimes awkward side of intimacy. I'm currently studying sex education, and my goal is simple: make these conversations feel normal, approachable, and maybe a little bit funny.

When I'm not in front of a camera, you'll probably find me working out, cooking something that takes way too long, or having a minor existential crisis in a new country (it happens to the best of us).
