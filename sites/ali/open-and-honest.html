The 3-Step Script

  • Step 1 — Validate: "I love our life and I'm so attracted to you."
  • Step 2 — Express (use "I"): "I feel like I have a side of me that wants to explore X, not because you aren't enough, but because I want to experience Y."
  • Step 3 — Safety Net: "I only want to do this if it brings us closer. If it hurts us, we stop."
📅

Homework

Module 3

The Safety Net — Jealousy & Aftercare

Creating a protocol for when feelings get hurt.

🎧

Audio Lesson: The Jealousy First-Aid Kit

Treating jealousy as a "Check Engine Light," not a Stop Sign.

✎

The "Gremlin" Protocol

  • When you feel jealous, text/say the code word (e.g., "Monkey")
  • Partner's job: Pause. Reassure. Do not get defensive.
  • Your job: Identify the specific fear — Am I being replaced? Do I feel left out?
✎

The Reconnection Ritual (Aftercare)

Define exactly what happens after a date with someone else — 10 minutes of cuddling, a shower together, or "don't tell me details until morning."

Module 4

The Hunt — Apps & Finding Partners

How to actually find people without being creepy or getting scammed.

🎧

Audio Lesson: The Meat Market

How to set up a profile on Feeld, Tinder, and local apps.

✎

The Profile Audit

  • Couples: Don't use a joint profile — it scares people. Link two separate profiles.
  • Photos: 1 face, 1 full body, 1 "vibe" shot (travel/lifestyle).
  • Bio: State exactly what you're looking for (e.g., "ENM couple looking for guest stars for travel").
✎

The 3-Question Vetting Screen

  • "Have you played with a couple/person in a relationship before?"
  • "What are your boundaries?"
  • "When was your last test?"
