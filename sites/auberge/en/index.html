Welcome to Les Éboulements

Nestled in one of Quebec's most beautiful villages, Auberge de nos Aïeux offers its visitors an incomparable view of the St. Lawrence River, Isle-aux-Coudres and the Charlevoix mountains. An enchanting and welcoming place for travellers, nomads and remote workers.
